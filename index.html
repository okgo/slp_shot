Welcome to Steve Lee ParsonS Shop

I have been a Firearms and Watch Collector since the age of 10 years.
We have a Winchester Rifle that has been passed down through five generations of my Family. I have always been an avid target shooter, and, as a US Army Veteran, I have had many years of training in the safe handling of firearms.

I have watches that date back to the 1880’s. I have always had a fascination with machines, and enjoy working on restoration of both watches and firearms. I have decided to turn my life’s interest into a new business venture.

This is why I went through the process of applying for both Federal and State Licenses to sell firearms.
